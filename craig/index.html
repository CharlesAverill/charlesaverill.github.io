This is a hack of Metroid for the NES I made when I was in high school. It's very buggy, sorry!

Your boss, Craig, hasn't given you the promotion he promised you over 8 months ago! After the divorce, losing visitation rights, and that jerk Chris that always messes up your Starbucks order, you've been driven to rage and have decided to take the one who caused it all (and all of his disgusting children) out, Office-Space style. Watch out for Time-Slowing and Invisible Kraids!

This hack blocks off access to all areas except for Kraid's Hideout. All enemies have been replaced with Mini-Kraid except for the original Mini-Kraid and Kraid. All main powerups are available early on to help with the killing spree. Game is complete after you kill Kraid and leave through the nearby elevator.
